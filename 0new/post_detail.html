Centre Internatiionaux Francophones des Lion Club de France 2019

  • Managment
  • 03 Comments

Dear friends,

Registration for the Francophone International Centers of the Lions Club of France 2019 is in progress. As you already know, each year our organization welcomes in France in July nearly 200 young French speakers from 18 to 25 years old from all over the world. If you receive this email today, it is because you have already registered for the Francophone International Centers but unfortunately your application could not be accepted. Thus, we invite you to renew your approach. Do not hesitate to take a few minutes to read carefully the steps to follow before applying. Registration is open until early March.

This year 7 Centers are organized:

CIFA (Center International Francophone Atlantique) in Parthenay, Thouars, Bressuire CIFARA (Center International Francophone Auvergne - Rhône - Alpes) in Annonay CIFC (Center International Francophone Culturel) in La Baule CIFGE (Center International Francophone Grand Est) in Strasbourg CIFCL (Center International Francophone Center Loire) in Orléans CIFUN (Center International Francophone Universitaire du Nord) in Lille You can contact us at contact@amicif.fr for any further information.

Friendships from France. Christian Gaillac, President of CIF Pierre Certenais, President of AMICIF and the entire Bureau des Anciens (BDA)
